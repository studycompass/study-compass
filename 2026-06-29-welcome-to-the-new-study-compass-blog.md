## A Smarter Way to Publish

We are thrilled to announce that the Study Compass blog is now powered by a professional Markdown-based publishing system. This means you can write, edit, and publish articles directly from your browser — no HTML coding, no file uploads, no technical knowledge required.

Every article you publish through the admin dashboard automatically appears on the blog homepage, gets its own SEO-friendly URL, and includes all the metadata search engines and social media platforms need to showcase your content.

## How It Works

The publishing system is built on three pillars:

### 1. Markdown Writing

Markdown is a simple formatting language that lets you write rich content without touching HTML. Use `#` for headings, `**bold**` for emphasis, `*italics*` for style, and `[links](url)` to connect to other pages. The CMS editor has a live preview so you can see exactly how your article will look before publishing.

### 2. Decap CMS Dashboard

Visit `/admin` in your browser, log in with your GitHub account, and you will see a clean dashboard listing all your blog posts. Click "New Post" to create an article, fill in the fields, and click "Publish." That is it — the article goes live instantly.

### 3. Automatic SEO

Every published article automatically generates:

- A clean, SEO-friendly URL (e.g., `/articles/your-article-title/`)
- Open Graph meta tags for Facebook and LinkedIn sharing
- Twitter Card meta tags for rich Twitter previews
- Canonical URLs to prevent duplicate content issues
- Structured data (JSON-LD) for Google rich results
- An auto-updated sitemap entry
- An RSS feed entry at `/feed.xml`

## Features at a Glance

- **Drafts:** Save work-in-progress articles without publishing them
- **Image uploads:** Drag and drop images directly into the editor
- **Categories and tags:** Organize articles by topic
- **Featured images:** Set a hero image for each article
- **Reading time:** Automatically calculated from word count
- **Related articles:** Shown at the bottom of each post
- **Search:** Built-in blog search powered by a Jekyll-generated index
- **RSS feed:** Auto-generated at `/feed.xml` for feed readers

## Getting Started

To publish your first article:

1. Visit `https://studycompass.github.io/study-compass/admin/`
2. Click "Login with GitHub" and authorize the app
3. Click "New Post" in the Blog Posts collection
4. Fill in the title, write your content in Markdown
5. Upload a featured image
6. Add categories and tags
7. Click "Publish"

Your article will be live on your website within seconds. No code, no file management, no deployment steps — just write and publish.
