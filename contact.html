About Study Compass

Study Compass is built by a small team of former students who got tired of cluttered, ad-stuffed study websites. We wanted a calm, beautifully designed space where any student — from high-school to grad school — could open a free Pomodoro timer, generate a quiz from their notes, or build a clean essay outline without signing up for anything.

Every tool here is crafted with focus in mind. We believe the right study environment changes everything — which is why the whole site adapts to four cozy themes (Morning, Night, Library, Nature) and remembers your favorite. Our premium planners and trackers, available on Payhip, fund the free tools so they can stay free forever.
